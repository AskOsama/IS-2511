🏆 ISO 27001 & PDCA

The PDCA cycle is fundamental to ISO 27001 certification. The standard requires organizations to:

  • Establish (Plan): Define the ISMS scope, policy, objectives, and processes
  • Implement (Do): Put the ISMS policies, controls, and processes into operation
  • Monitor (Check): Assess and measure performance against the ISMS policy
  • Maintain (Act): Take corrective and preventive actions based on audits and reviews

Key Point: ISO 27001:2022 emphasizes a risk-based approach where the PDCA cycle drives continuous improvement of the Information Security Management System.
